My Top 3 Series of All Time.

Money Hiest

When the national mint and a touring school group are held hostage by robbers, police believe that the thieves have no way out. Little do they know that the thieves have a bigger plan in store.

Berlin

Berlin, the Money Heist spinoff, will return to Netflix for a second season. “The iconic character, played by Pedro Alonso, will be back with a new heist and more love,” the streamer said Monday. Berlin's season one gang will be back for season two.

Stranger Things

In 1980s Indiana, a group of young friends witness supernatural forces and secret government exploits. As they search for answers, the children unravel a series of extraordinary mysteries.
